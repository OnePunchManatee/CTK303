What is TBT?

The Bad Touch is an inactive competitive Team Fortress 2 team comprised of 1 decent player and 10-12 shitters. Due to caprice and shifting obligations, the team's main roster fluctuates wildly from season to season, with each season featuring at least one new player, or the return of an old player, and multiple class/role exchanges.

Who's on the team?

The team is an eclectic collection of TF2 players who were generally either

roped in by friends or slipped down the MGE-to-Comp Pipeline.

Has It Got Any Sports In It?

Pfah, are you kidding? Demoknights, midfights, last holds, revenge crits, heavies,

phlog pyros, chases, escapes, true love, miracles!
